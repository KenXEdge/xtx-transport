Our Services

Professional freight transportation solutions for shippers, brokers, and logistics partners across the United States.

Core Services

Transportation Solutions

XTX Transport is authorized to haul general freight across the USA. Here's what we specialize in:

General Freight

Full truckload (FTL) general freight hauling for all legal commodities. We handle consumer goods, industrial equipment, packaged products, and more — securely loaded, properly documented, and delivered on schedule.

  • Consumer goods & retail products
  • Industrial & commercial equipment
  • Packaged and palletized freight
  • E-commerce & fulfillment loads
Interstate Transport

FMCSA-authorized interstate carrier ready to move your freight from Texas to any of the 48 contiguous states. Long-haul, mid-range, or regional — we operate where you need us most.

  • Active for-hire interstate authority
  • All 48 contiguous states covered
  • Texas to nationwide corridors
  • Long-haul & regional routes
Dedicated Loads

Exclusive capacity commitments for brokers and shippers who need a reliable, recurring carrier partner. Dedicated truck, dedicated driver, dedicated service — no surprises, no double brokering.

  • Committed weekly capacity
  • Lane-specific agreements
  • Direct carrier relationship
  • Consistent, predictable service
Regional Hauls

Specialized in Texas and Southwest regional corridors including Dallas-Fort Worth metro, Houston, San Antonio, and beyond. Fast turnaround regional service for time-sensitive loads.

  • DFW metro area specialist
  • Texas statewide coverage
  • Southwest regional lanes
  • Fast turnaround service
Broker Partnerships

We actively work with freight brokers and 3PLs. Our clean compliance record, active authority, and professional communication make us a top-tier carrier partner for your network.

  • Active on DAT & load boards
  • Quick confirmation & check calls
  • Proper insurance certificates
  • Responsive POD/BOL handling
White Glove Service

For high-value or high-priority loads that demand extra care and attention. Direct communication from pickup to delivery, with real-time status updates and immediate issue resolution.

  • Direct driver communication
  • Real-time load tracking updates
  • Priority load handling
  • Immediate issue resolution
