Zero Tolerance Safety Culture

Safety is never optional at XTX Transport. Pre-trip and post-trip inspections are conducted for every load. Vehicle maintenance is performed on schedule. We don't cut corners — period.

ELD & HOS Compliance

Fully compliant with FMCSA's Electronic Logging Device (ELD) mandate. Hours of Service (HOS) regulations are strictly followed. No falsified logs, no exceptions.

Drug & Alcohol Program

All drivers enrolled in a FMCSA-compliant drug and alcohol testing consortium. Pre-employment, random, and post-accident testing conducted per DOT regulations.

Insurance Coverage

Proper liability, cargo, and physical damage insurance maintained at all times. Certificates of insurance available upon request for broker and shipper qualification.
