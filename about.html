Who We Are

XTX LLC — Our Story

XTX Transport, operating as XTX LLC, is an independent interstate trucking company headquartered in Fort Worth, Texas. We are fully registered with the FMCSA (Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration) with an active for-hire authority, licensed to haul general freight across the United States.

Founded with a commitment to excellence, we maintain one of the industry's cleanest compliance records — a 0% out-of-service rate for both vehicles and drivers, and zero crashes in the last 24 months. Our approach is simple: do things right, every single time.

  • Interstate carrier — authorized in all 48 contiguous states
  • Fully insured for cargo and liability
  • Professional, CDL-certified drivers
  • Compliance-first culture and operations
  • Direct communication — no middlemen
